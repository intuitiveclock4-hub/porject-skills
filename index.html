What we researched

This project combined secondary research with a student survey to identify what matters most for wellbeing at school.

Secondary Research

Mindmaps and visual secondary-source material.

Primary Research

Survey charts from real student responses.

Design Ideas

Prototype ideas: posters, app concepts, and wellbeing support hub layouts.

Key findings

Stress levels

Most students often feel stressed due to tests and workload.

Support availability

Many were unsure of the support options available.

What helps most

Talking to someone, sleep, and routines were the most helpful coping skills.

Student suggestions

More clubs, flexible deadlines, wellbeing breaks, and a student support hub.
