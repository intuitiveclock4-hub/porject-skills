What we researched

This project combined secondary research with a student survey to identify what matters most for wellbeing at school.

Secondary research

Summary of literature and reputable resources on mental health and positive school environments.

Primary research

A Google Form collected student responses about stress, support and improvements.

Design ideas

Prototype ideas: app, posters and a dedicated support hub.

Key findings

Stress levels

Most students reported feeling sometimes to often stressed. Common causes were workload and tests.

Support availability

Many students felt unsure whether their school provides enough mental health support.

What helps most

Sleep, talking to someone and structured routines were top coping strategies.

Student suggestions

More clubs, flexible deadlines, wellbeing breaks and a visible Student Support Hub.

Resources & ideas

Support Hub

Create a page with counselling info, helplines, self-help and recommended apps.

Events & clubs

Field day, movie nights, baking, music, sport and student-run socials.

Poster & app ideas

Use consistent colours and simple infographics to explain support services.
