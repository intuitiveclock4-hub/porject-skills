What we researched

This project combined secondary research with a student survey to identify what matters most for wellbeing at school.

Secondary Research

Key findings gathered from reputable websites and mental health sources.

Primary Research

We collected data from real students through a Google Form.

Design Ideas

Prototype designs were made to support student wellbeing through visuals and app ideas.

Key findings

Stress levels

Most students reported feeling sometimes to often stressed.

Support availability

Many students felt unsure whether enough support exists.

Helpful strategies

Talking to someone, rest and structure were helpful.

Student suggestions

More clubs, flexible deadlines and wellbeing hubs.
